How to Achieve a Normal BMI


1. Portion Control

Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating. A serving is an exact amount of food. To better manage what you're eating, you could carry around measuring cups and spoons.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Most healthy people can stay hydrated by drinking water and other fluids whenever they feel thirsty. Fewer eight glasses a day might be enough.

3. Regular Exercise

Engage in physical activities such as walking, running, or strength training for at least 150 minutes a week.

4. Consult a Healthcare Provider

Seek professional advice for personalized recommendations.
