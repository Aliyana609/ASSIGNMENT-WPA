About Us

Medi by Lyn is a health-focused service founded and operated by experienced healthcare professionals. With over 20 years of combined experience in the healthcare field, our mission is to provide personalized care that addresses the unique needs of each patient, especially in managing their Body Mass Index (BMI).

Our Motto
"Your health, our priority" reflects our dedication to offering compassionate and comprehensive care. We are committed to excellence in patient care, emphasizing the importance of managing BMI and promoting overall health through tailored support. We offer a wide range of services, including physical, occupational, and speech therapy, skilled nursing, and home health aide services. Our team also includes medical social workers who assist with individualized care plans and resources. At Medi by Lyn, we focus on helping our patients achieve and maintain a healthy BMI, alongside addressing other medical needs.
